How fees are ranked:

Finflow-X's fee levels are ranked as low, average, or high based on a comparison with other reviewed brokers. Trading fees and non-trading fees are taken into account.

Trading fees include commissions, spreads, financing rates, and conversion fees. Non-trading fees include charges unrelated to trading, such as withdrawal fees or inactivity fees.

Comparisons are made with similar brokers, based on objective factors like product offerings, client profile, and fee structure.

Trading Fees:

Finflow-X's trading fees are considered low. The pricing structure depends on the chosen account type, with the review focusing on the FxPro Platform account.

Account Base Currencies:

At Finflow-X, you have the option to choose from 8 base currencies, including USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, PLN, AUD, JPY, and ZAR. While this selection is average compared to similar brokers, it is worth noting that only a few minor currencies are accepted.

Why does this matter? If you fund your trading account in the same currency as your bank account or trade assets in the same currency as your trading account base currency, you can avoid paying conversion fees.

Deposit Fees and Options:

Finflow-X does not charge any deposit fees. Alongside bank transfers and credit/debit cards, you can utilize several types of electronic wallets, including PayPal, Skrill, and Neteller. However, please note that electronic wallets are not available for UK clients.

Withdrawal Fees and Options:

Finflow-X generally does not impose withdrawal fees. However, there are exceptions. A fee is applicable if you refund to your PayPal account more than 6 months after the initial funding or if you refund to Neteller or Skrill without having executed any trades.

How long does it take to withdraw money from Finflow-X? In our testing, credit card/bank transfer withdrawals took 1 business day.

Please note that withdrawals can only be made to accounts in your name.

How to Withdraw Money from Finflow-X:

To withdraw money from your Finflow-X account, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account (Finflow-X Direct).
  • Go to the 'Wallet' section.
  • Click on 'Withdrawal.'
  • Select your preferred withdrawal method.
  • Enter the withdrawal amount.

Overall, Finflow-X excels in providing free and efficient deposit and withdrawal options through a variety of channels. The availability of credit/debit cards and the absence of deposit fees are significant advantages. However, the limitation on minor currencies may be a drawback for some traders.

Supported Languages on the Mobile Trading Platform:

The mobile trading platform on Finflow-X supports multiple languages, similar to the web version. For iOS, the language options are the same as those available on the web version. On Android, the platform uses the default language of the user's phone.

Look and Feel:

The Finflow-X MT4 mobile trading platform features a great-looking design that is visually appealing. The platform is well organized and user-friendly, allowing users to easily navigate and access all the necessary features.

Login and Security:

Finflow-X provides the option for two-step login on the MT4 mobile trading platform, which adds an extra layer of security to the login process. However, it does not support Touch/Face ID login, which would provide greater convenience for users.

Search Functions:

The search functions on the mobile trading platform are effective, allowing users to search for specific products by typing their names or by navigating through category folders. This makes it easier to locate and trade desired assets.

Placing Orders:

Users can utilize the same order types and order time limits on the mobile trading platform as they would on the web trading platform. This includes market, limit, stop, and trailing stop orders, as well as order time limits like Good 'til Canceled (GTC) and Good 'til Time (GTT).

Alerts and Notifications:

While alerts and notifications can be set for mobile devices, this functionality is only available on the desktop trading platform. It would be more convenient if users could set alerts and notifications directly from the mobile trading platform itself.

Overall, the Finflow-X mobile trading platform, particularly on mobile, offers a well-designed and user-friendly experience. The platform's search function is effective, and the option for two-step login enhances security. However, the lack of Touch/Face ID login, order confirmation, and full alerts and notifications functionality on the mobile platform may be considered drawbacks.
